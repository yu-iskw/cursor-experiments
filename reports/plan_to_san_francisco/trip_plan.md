## Accommodation

*Research provided by @hotel-researcher*

We have selected the **Chancellor Hotel on Union Square** for its central location, classic San Francisco charm, and excellent value for a standard budget.

* **Hotel Name:** Chancellor Hotel on Union Square
* **Location:** 433 Powell St, San Francisco, CA 94102
* **Estimated Price:** ~$155 per night (Total for 7 nights: ~$1,085)
* **Key Amenities:** Complimentary bottled water, umbrella use, and central access to the Cable Car line directly outside the door.

## Sample Day-by-Day Itinerary

| Day | Theme | Key Activities |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Day 1** | Arrival | Arrive at SFO, take BART or rideshare to Union Square. Evening walk around the shops and dinner in Maiden Lane. |
| **Day 2** | Icons of the Bay | Morning visit to the **Golden Gate Bridge**. Walk across to Vista Point. Afternoon at the **Palace of Fine Arts** and Crissy Field. |
| **Day 3** | History & Piers | Take the ferry to **Alcatraz Island** (booking in advance is required). Lunch at **Fisherman's Wharf** and see the sea lions at Pier 39. |
| **Day 4** | Park & Culture | Full day at **Golden Gate Park**. Visit the **Japanese Tea Garden**, de Young Museum, and the California Academy of Sciences. |
| **Day 5** | Foodie Tour | Start at the **Ferry Building Marketplace** for breakfast. Walk the Embarcadero to **Coit Tower** for 360-degree city views. |
| **Day 6** | Local Vibes | Explore the **Mission District** murals. Grab a burrito at La Taqueria and relax at **Dolores Park** before walking through the Castro. |
| **Day 7** | Steep Streets | Ride the Cable Car to **Lombard Street** (the "crookedest" street). Explore **Chinatown** and have a farewell dinner in **North Beach**. |
| **Day 8** | Departure | Last-minute souvenir shopping at Union Square. Brunch at a local cafe before heading back to SFO for your afternoon flight. |
